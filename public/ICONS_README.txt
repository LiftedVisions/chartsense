PLACEHOLDER — Replace these files with real icons before deploying.

Required:
- icon-192.png  (192x192px PNG)
- icon-512.png  (512x512px PNG)

Quick way to generate:
1. Go to https://favicon.io or https://realfavicongenerator.net
2. Upload a logo or use text "CS" with accent blue #4f8ef7 background
3. Download and drop icon-192.png and icon-512.png into this folder

These are referenced in:
- public/manifest.json
- index.html (apple-touch-icon)
- vite.config.ts (includeAssets)
